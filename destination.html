Hill station in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India known as the "Queen of Hills" and is India's most famous hill station. Explore

Jammu & Kashmir , Srinagar

Jammu and Kashmir, a union territory in India, is known for its stunning landscapes, including the Kashmir Valley and the Jammu region, and is a popular tourist destination, also known as "Paradise on Earth". Explore

Dehradun , Uttarakhand

Dehradun also known as Dehra Doon, is the winter capital and the most populous city of the Indian state of Uttarakhand Explore

New Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India . A vibrant metropolis with a blend of ancient and modern, with historical monuments alongside bustling markets and modern infrastructure. Explore

Jaipur , Rajasthan

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, India, is known as the "Pink City" and is a major tourist destination renowned for its historical sites Explore

Madhya Pradesh , Bhopal

Madhya Pradesh, meaning "Central Province" and known as the "Heart of India," is a state in central India, rich in culture, history, and natural resources, with a diverse landscape and a significant number of monuments and wildlife sanctuaries. Explore

Gujarat , Gandhinagar

Gujarat, a state in western India on the Arabian Sea, boasts a rich history, diverse culture, and a significant coastline, including the world's largest ship scrapyard Explore

Haryana , Chandigarh

Haryana a state in northern India, was formed in 1966 from parts of Punjab and is known for its rich history, agricultural significance, and proximity to the National Capital Region Explore

Uttar Pradesh , Lucknow

Uttar Pradesh, a state in northern India, is the most populous state in India and is known for its rich history, culture, and religious significance, including the Taj Mahal and the cities of Varanasi and Ayodhya. Explore

Bihar , Patna

Bihar, a state in Eastern India, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and is the birthplace of Buddhism and the Mauryan emperor Ashoka. It's also known for its diverse religions and languages Explore

Jharkhand , Ranchi

Jharkhand, meaning "land of forests," is a state in eastern India, known for its rich mineral resources, diverse culture, and tribal communities. Explore

Odisha , Bhubaneswar

Odisha, formerly Orissa, is a state in eastern India, known for its ancient temples, vibrant culture, and diverse landscapes, including beaches, forests, and rivers. Explore

Chattisgarh , Raipur

Chhattisgarh, a state in central India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and tribal communities, and is a region known for its rice production and mineral resources. Explore

Maharashtra , Mumbai

Maharashtra, known as the "gateway of India," is a state in western India, boasting a rich history, vibrant culture, and significant commercial and industrial activity, including famous cave paintings at Ajanta and Ellora. Explore

South India

Kerala ,Kochi

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a state in southwestern India characterized by lush landscapes, scenic backwaters, and a rich cultural heritage, with a coastline along the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats mountains. Explore

Karnataka ,Bangalore

Karnataka is known for its diverse landscapes, rich history, and thriving IT industry, encompassing everything from the Western Ghats to the coastal plains, and is home to ancient ruins, royal palaces, and modern technology hubs. Explore

Tamil Nadu , Chennai

Karnataka is known for its diverse landscapes, rich history, and thriving IT industry, encompassing everything from the Western Ghats to the coastal plains, and is home to ancient ruins, royal palaces, and modern technology hubs. Explore

Andhra Pradesh , Hyderabad

Andhra Pradesh, a state in southeastern India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse cuisine, and its long coastline along the Bay of Bengal, with Amaravati serving as its capital. Explore

Telangana , Hyderabad

Telangana, formed in 2014 as India's 29th state, located in the south-central part of the Indian subcontinent on the Deccan Plateau, is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and a rapidly developing economy. Explore

Goa , Panaji

Goa, a state on India's southwestern coast, is known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture, particularly its Portuguese colonial heritage Explore

North-East India

Arunachal Pradesh , Itanagar

Arunachal Pradesh, known as the "Land of the Dawn-lit Mountains," is a northeastern Indian state characterized by its stunning, mountainous terrain, diverse flora and fauna, and vibrant tribal cultures Explore

Assam , Dispur

The State is one of the richest biodiversity zones in the world and consists of tropical rainforests, deciduous forests, riverine grasslands, bamboo orchards, and numerous wetland ecosystems. Explore

Manipur , Imphal

Manipur, a state in northeastern India, is known for its rich culture, natural beauty, and unique traditions, including its famous classical dance, Raas Leela, and its role as the birthplace of modern polo Explore

Meghalaya , Shillong

Meghalaya, meaning "abode of the clouds" in Sanskrit, is a state in northeastern India known for its lush landscapes, heavy rainfall, and unique tribal cultures, particularly those of the Khasi, Jaintia, and Garo people. Explore

Mizoram , Aizawl

Mizoram, a mountainous state in northeastern India, is known for its rolling hills, dense forests, alleys, rivers, lakes and rich cultural heritage Explore

Nagaland , Kohima

Nagaland, located in the northeastern part of India, is a land of diverse cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant festivals, known as the "Land of Festivals" characterized by mountainous terrain, numerous tribes, and a rich cultural heritage. Explore

Tripura, Agartala

Tripura is a state in northeastern India, known for its hilly terrain, rich cultural heritage, and diverse tribal communities Explore

Sikkim , Gangtok

Sikkim, a small state, is known for its stunning Himalayan landscapes, rich biodiversity, and unique cultural heritage, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Explore

West Bengal , Kolkata

West Bengal, a state in eastern India, encompasses diverse landscapes, a rich cultural heritage, and a significant population, including the Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage site with the world's largest mangrove forest. Explore
